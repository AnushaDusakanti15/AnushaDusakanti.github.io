REDESIGNING PROJECT

Below is a redesigning project on which my classmate, Sumrah Shakeel and I worked on for the Applied Statistics & Visualization for Analytics class taken at George Mason University. This project involves us finding a bad graph and redesigning it to display meaningful visualizations. The graph we selected to redesign is a line chart that shows the Unemployment Rate from 1968-2022 in Japan. The amount of information on this graph is quite a lot and the viewer can barely pinpoint the percentages mentioned here.

Visualization 1: Grouped Bar Chart for Unemployment Rate during Ice Age (1994-2004) in Japan
Visualization 2: Multi-line plot with facetswhere each facet represents different sexes and each line represents a trend over the Ice Age (1994-2004) in Japan
Visualization 3: Line Chart showing Relationship between GDP Growth Rate and Unemployment Rate in Japan
Visualization 4: World map with unemployment rate of different countries in 2022
